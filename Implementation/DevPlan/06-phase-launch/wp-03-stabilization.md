# Launch WP-03 Stabilization

## Goal

Stabilize production operation and close launch period.

## Tasks

T1. Early-life monitoring
- T1.1 Track critical metrics and alerts by shift window.
- T1.2 Classify incidents and assign owners.

T2. Corrective actions
- T2.1 Execute prioritized fixes for critical and high incidents.
- T2.2 Verify corrective action outcomes.

T3. Launch closure
- T3.1 Produce stabilization summary.
- T3.2 Transfer ownership to steady-state operations.

## Deliverables

- Stabilization report.
- Known issues register.

## Acceptance criteria

- No unresolved critical incidents remain at launch closure decision.
