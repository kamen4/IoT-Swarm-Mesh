# Launch WP-01 Production Readiness

## Goal

Ensure operational and governance readiness before production cutover.

## Tasks

T1. Readiness checklist completion
- T1.1 Confirm all phase-gate evidence is archived.
- T1.2 Confirm runbooks are complete and owned.

T2. Operational readiness
- T2.1 Confirm monitoring and alerting coverage.
- T2.2 Confirm incident response ownership and escalation paths.

T3. Data and configuration readiness
- T3.1 Confirm baseline parameter profile availability.
- T3.2 Confirm configuration management process.

## Deliverables

- Production readiness report.
- Signed readiness checklist.

## Acceptance criteria

- All mandatory readiness items are complete and approved.
